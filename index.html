Getting there

Travel & stay

Coming in from out of town? Here's a little help for the journey.

Driving in

Kurunegala is roughly a 2-hour drive from Colombo via the Kandy Road (A1). Parking is available at the venue. Tap the maps button above for live directions from wherever you are.

View venue on the map →

Staying over

If you'd rather not drive back the same day, there are hotels and guesthouses around Kurunegala town, a short ride from the venue. We're happy to help you find a room — just ask.

Tip: book early, August is a popular season.
